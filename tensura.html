Plot Summary:


Lonely thirty-seven-year-old Satoru Mikami is stuck in a dead-end job, unhappy with his mundane life, but after dying at the hands of a robber, he awakens to a fresh start in a fantasy realm...as a slime monster! As he acclimates to his goopy new existence, his exploits with the other monsters set off a chain of events that will change his new world forever!

Reviews

Rating: ★★★★☆

Man, this gets amazing volume after volume, although I read in English translation so I likely would have stopped after Vol.16 (which is the latest official translation till today) but I moved further and read fan translations and even Vol.20, which I was not able to find even the fan one, I read the one which gets translated by the AI or different apps. Yea it gets a bit boring during the Founder festival arc considering the whole story but after that it stars to get amazing and thrilling bit by bit (excluding Vol. 17). I really hope even if the story of Rimuru ends the author will continue to write the stories of other characters in the Tensura world indulging our privious characters to make things little spicy.

Rating: ★★★☆☆

The writing style of the series is light-hearted and entertaining, with plenty of humor and action scenes to keep readers engaged. The world-building is well-developed and imaginative, and readers will appreciate the attention to detail that has been put into creating the various factions and cultures within the world. The main character, Rimuru, is a likable and relatable protagonist, and readers will enjoy following his journey as he grows and develops throughout the series. The supporting characters are also well-written and add depth and complexity to the story. Overall, "I Reincarnated as a Slime" is an excellent choice for readers who enjoy fantasy, comedy, and action-packed adventures. The series is well-written, engaging, and has a unique premise that sets it apart from other light novels. Highly recommended for fans of the genre.
