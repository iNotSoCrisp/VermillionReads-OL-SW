Plot Summary:


King Grey has unrivaled strength, wealth, and prestige in a world governed by martial ability. However, solitude lingers closely behind those with great power. Beneath the glamorous exterior of a powerful king lurks the shell of man, devoid of purpose and will. Reincarnated into a new world filled with magic and monsters, the king has a second chance to relive his life. Correcting the mistakes of his past will not be his only challenge, however. Underneath the peace and prosperity of the new world is an undercurrent threatening to destroy everything he has worked for, questioning his role and reason for being born again

Reviews

Rating: ★★★★☆

The story becomes epic. When you are at chapter 175 and as a webnovel reader you know that the action is only really starting (chapters correspond to 1/4 of the current webnovel chapters)! I’m really looking forward to the Horny Trio: Grey, Regis and Caera who will replace the current trio Arthur, Sylvie and Tessia. But unfortunately that won’t happen until after about chapter 400. The author doesn’t seem to like elves, because they’re really going to bleed in the future.

Rating: ★★★☆☆

So, so, so good! No silly drama, no silly conflicts, good world building, good art, good character growth. The more silly and lighthearted parts are justified and make sense. Great battles. Just read it, damn it!
