Plot Summary:


Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint follows MC as he attempts to survive in the midst of a crumbling world. When fictions comes to reality, there is little he can do outside of following the story himself. However, with the knowledge of the present and future, MC decides that he must survive. In doing so, MC begins to change the story himself. As the fate of the world begins to deviate from its outlined path, MC must take it upon himself to risk the life he holds dearly for the good of humanity.
